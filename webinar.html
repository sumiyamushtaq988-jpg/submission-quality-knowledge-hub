Submission-Quality Discipline: Preventing the Deficiencies That Delay ANDA Approval

A practical training session on building the submission-quality discipline that helps applicant-side teams prevent first-cycle deficiencies and reduce avoidable FDA review delays.

Recording coming soon The webinar replay will appear here once it's published.

What this session covers

This training looks at submission quality as a repeatable discipline rather than a one-time scramble before filing. The focus is preventive and applicant-side: the habits, checks, and dossier reviews that keep avoidable deficiencies out of your ANDA.

Learning objectives

  • Recognize the recurring, preventable issues that most often trigger first-cycle deficiencies.
  • Apply a pre-filing completeness mindset across CTD modules before submission.
  • Build simple internal self-audit checkpoints into your submission workflow.
  • Use FDA primary sources effectively rather than relying on second-hand summaries.
