About the author

Submission-Quality Knowledge Hub is an independent professional-development initiative led by Sumiya Mushtaq, PharmD, MBA, sharing applicant-side submission-quality expertise with the wider regulatory community.

Sumiya Mushtaq, PharmD, MBA

Sumiya Mushtaq is a regulatory-affairs professional with roughly ten years of ICH-CTD / ANDA submission-quality experience on the manufacturer (applicant) side. Her work has centered on preparing and strengthening generic-drug submissions — building complete, well-organized CTD dossiers and catching the preventable gaps that lead to first-cycle deficiencies and avoidable FDA review delays.

This knowledge hub exists to transfer that submission-quality expertise to the wider regulatory community. The goal is practical and field-level: help RA, CMC, and QA professionals raise the quality of what they file, so preventable review delays go down across the industry.
